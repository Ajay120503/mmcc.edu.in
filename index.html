Vision

To impart quality education for professional excellence and sustainable development through continuous improvement, teamwork and efforts so as to promote welfare to all our stakeholders.

Mission

MMCC will strive to offer opportunities for learning and development of our students and work towards the attainment of social justice, equity and contentment for all our stakeholders.
